About me

Two years ago, after many years of working in commercial positions, I decided to change my work to a more interesting and developmental one.
In college, I had the opportunity to come into contact with programming for a moment, which is why I assumed, that I would enjoy working in IT. Searching for a field in which I could best realize myself, I came across a front-end, that captivated me, because it requires a lot of creativity and the visual effects of work can be seen very quickly. I decided to start bootcamp, preparing to work as a web developer. During the course I had no doubt, that creating websites and web applications is just something I would like to do in my life!
Since then I've been trying to constantly develop, learn new technologies and current trends, create my own projects. Learning programming gives me incredible satisfaction all the time, and every completed project invariably encourages me to quickly create another, even better.
